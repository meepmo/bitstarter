NOT JUST ANOTHER SOCIAL NETWORK

for the kids

We've carved out a secure nook on the web just for kids. You control who you share your child's profile with. Save artwork and projects, log milestones, and more! No more piggybacking on your profile or random lurkers viewing baby's pictures on your page.

no to hunger

Whether or not you create a page for your child, you can be part of the solution to hunger and food insecurity with Chance for Change. Start or join promote food recovery initiatives, popup farmer's markets, communal edible gardens, and more!

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What's a bubhubs page?
It looks like a regular social networking profile, but the bubhubs are specially designed with the safety of the children's internet identities in mind. Now you can feel safe posting photos and videos of your children and sharing them with your family and friends.
If I create a bubhubs page for my child, who can view it?
We value you and your child's privacy, so only the people you personally invite would be able to see it. You can even change the privacy settings in each section of the page!
I can save artwork and projects to my child's profile? Tell me more!
You don't have to feel guilty if you accidentally throw away your child's latest watercolor masterpiece. You can take a picture of their artwork, or a 360°F picture of their school project!
Can I import photo albums I have of my child that's on my Facebook page?
Yes! You can easily move any photos and albums from Facebook to your hub. As an added safety feature, you can also set the privacy settings on each album on your hub.
What happens when I don't want to maintain a profile for my child anymore?
You can choose to download the entire profile and save a readable offline copy for your safekeeping if you linke. When you choose to delete your child's profile, no traces of it will be left on our network. You can choose to keep your account active if you wish to stay involved in the community sustainability programs we have.
What's the Chance for Change page?
Whether or not you choose to create a profile for your kids, you can still sign up to help do your part in fixing the problem of hunger and food insecurity in your community and even beyond. In the United States alone, 1 in 4 children don't know where their next meal will be coming from. Chance for Change is a venue for those of us who want to create and implement solutions to this very serious problem.
I don't have kids, so I don't need to create a bubhubs page. Can I still be part of Chance for Change?
You are absolutely welcome to sign up for the Chance for Change page. In fact, we encourage you to spread the word to everyone you know! We need all the help we can get to ensure that no child goes hungry and that our future generation understands the importance of a healthy and sustainable way of life.
How are the collected funds going to be used?
We're bootstrapping this project so we can get things rolling as fast as we can. We feel strongly about protecting our children's identities on the web and about ensuring that no child goes hungry if we can help it. We need your help to make this happen!
Why should I sign up?

It's free, for starters! The information you share on your bubhubs profile is safe, secure and 100% yours. We will not use your personal information nor share it without your permission. Most importantly, you can delete your account in one simple step, leaving absolutely zero web crumbs.

And if you sign up at Chance for Change, you have the opportunity to stand up to the problem of hunger and food insecurity. No child should ever go hungry, or have their health suffer from insufficient nutrition and an unhealthy diet.
